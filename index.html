About

I’m a 4th-year PhD candidate studying musculoskeletal mechanobiology with an emphasis on injury-driven inflammation, aging, and sex-dependent repair.

  • Modeling physiologic and targeted tissue injury
  • Quantifying cytokines/proteins and tissue structure/function
  • Translational interest in precision biologics & delivery

Research

Bone–tendon–muscle crosstalk
How injury in one tissue drives secondary damage and altered repair elsewhere.
Age & sex effects on inflammation
Mapping pro-/anti-inflammatory signals and functional outcomes across time.
Therapeutic design direction
Data-guided strategies for targeted cytokine modulation and delivery.
